Effective Date: 2024-07-10

1. Introduction

Welcome to MineAware VR. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our VR app.

We do not collect any personal or non-personal data from users of the MineAware VR app. We respect your privacy and have designed our app to ensure that your information remains secure and private.

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use, share, or disclose any personal or non-personal data from our users.

MineAware VR does not integrate with any third-party services that collect, store, or use data from our users. Your usage of the app is entirely private and secure.

We do not collect or store any information from your device. Any data generated by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

While we do not collect any data, we are committed to maintaining the security and integrity of our app to ensure a safe user experience.
