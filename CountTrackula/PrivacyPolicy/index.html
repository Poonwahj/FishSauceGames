Privacy Policy for CountTrackula

Effective Date: 2025-01-01

1. Introduction

Welcome to CountTrackula. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CountTrackula handles your data when you use our progressive web app (PWA).

2. Data Storage

CountTrackula stores all of your tracking data locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. We do not operate servers that collect, receive, or store your personal information.

If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, your data is stored in your own Google Drive account under a folder created by CountTrackula. Fish Sauce Games does not have access to your Google Drive or the data stored within it.

3. Google Drive Integration

CountTrackula offers optional integration with Google Drive to synchronize your data across devices. When you enable this feature:

  • You will be asked to authorize CountTrackula to access a specific folder in your Google Drive.
  • Your tracking data is written directly to your Google Drive by your browser — it does not pass through our servers.
  • You can revoke this access at any time through your Google Account settings.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

CountTrackula does not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your health, fitness, or nutrition data
  • Your location
  • Device identifiers or analytics

5. Data Export

CountTrackula allows you to export your data as JSON. This file stays on your device and is never transmitted to Fish Sauce Games.

6. Third-Party Services

The only optional third-party integration is Google Drive, described above. CountTrackula does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or any other third-party data processors.

7. Children's Privacy

CountTrackula is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
